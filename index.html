RIVENA

Rivena is the ethereal voice behind a new wave of alt pop
blending witchy aesthetics, Celtic rock undertones, and
survivor-driven storytelling into a hauntingly emotional
sound. Rivena’s music moves like a spell, honest, raw, and
beautifully unafraid. Her debut album, Trigger Game,
explores cycles of manipulation, emotional abuse, survival,
and reclaimed power. Each song is both a confession and
an anthem, designed to awaken the part of you that
refused to stay broken.
